Meet Our Performers

Arjith singh

Arijit Singh is a celebrated Indian playback singer, famous for his heartfelt and soulful voice. his versatile singing style has made him one of the most popular artist. With numerous awards to his name, Arijit continues to captivate audiences with his powerful and emotional performances.

Shreya Goshal

Shreya Ghoshal is a renowned Indian playback singer celebrated for her soulful voice and versatility across multiple languages. With numerous awards and timeless hits, she has captivated audiences for over two decades, making her a beloved icon in Indian music.

Anirudh Ravichander

Anirudh Ravichander is a celebrated Indian composer and singer. His innovative fusion of Indian and global music styles has made him a leading figure in Tamil cinema. With energetic performances and a fresh approach, Anirudh is redefining modern Indian music.
